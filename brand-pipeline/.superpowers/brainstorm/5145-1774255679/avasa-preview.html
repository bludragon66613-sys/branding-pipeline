Typeface shift — Cormorant Garamond (flowing, literary) → Bodoni Moda (high-contrast, engraved, banking-house authority). Thicker/thinner stroke tension reads as vault-door precision.
Accent shift — #B8866A (warm terracotta copper, shared by 8 brands) → #8C4B2A (cognac sienna, deeper red-brown, distinct from any other brand's warm).
Cross-hatch motif — Subtle diamond engraving pattern on concept slide (S03). References old-money engraving / banknote heritage. Unique to The Vault archetype.
Tone shift — Void / Permanence / Copper warmth → Authority / Inheritance / Cognac. Language now locks in the "vault" territory so no other brand can occupy it.
