About Mark
Mark is a company of precision. Every dimension, every material, every decision is documented, deliberated, and defended. We do not build impressively. We build correctly. Sthāna is the residential expression of that discipline — twelve villas in Jubilee Hills, designed with an engineer's exactitude and finished with an architect's restraint.

Exclusivity
No shared walls. No shared lobbies. No shared anything.

Materials specified. Materials delivered.
