## Platform Strategy

**Instagram** is the primary stage. Every piece of content is conceived for Instagram first — the grain of the image, the pace of the reel, the weight of the caption. The grid should read as a single, cohesive object: dark stone, pale light, sustained stillness.

**LinkedIn** is the business tier — same story, different register. Captions shift from sensory to considered. The audience here is the HNI investor, the NRI evaluating a return, the family office. We address their intelligence, not their aspiration.

**Voice across all platforms:** One sentence that earns its place. No adjective without purpose. No superlative. Silence is not the absence of content — it is the content.

# DAY 1 — D-9

## Theme: Stone. Before the name.

**Format:** Reel · 15 seconds · 9:16 vertical

---

### Feed Caption

Some things are not announced.

They are found.

---

### Visual Direction

The reel is a single, sustained shot — no cuts, no music. A slab of raw basalt or dark granite, wet from morning dew. The camera starts extreme close-up on surface texture — pores, grain, mineral variation. Over 15 seconds, it pulls back at a glacial pace, revealing almost nothing more: just the scale of the stone expanding into frame. No sky, no structure, no logo. The final frame holds on the stone's edge meeting darkness.

Sound: ambient stone quarry resonance — low, subterranean hum — then silence. No voiceover. No music bed. The sound design IS the experience.

Aspect ratio: 9:16 for Reels. Also export a 1:1 crop of the final frame for the grid thumbnail. Grid should appear nearly black.

No supers. No text. No logo. No handle watermark on this post.

---

### Story Content

One story only. Black screen. White text, centered, in the brand serif:

**"Coming."**

Below it, a poll sticker:

> *Do you know what this is?*
> — Stone.
> — Something else.

No follow-up. No reply. The story disappears in 24 hours.

---

### Engagement Mechanic

The poll is the mechanic. It generates curiosity data and surfaces the account to non-followers via the algorithm. Do not respond to DMs today. The silence is intentional.

---

### LinkedIn Adaptation

No LinkedIn post on Day 1. The teaser works only in the Instagram context. LinkedIn entry begins Day 4.

## Post-Campaign Note

The ten-day campaign ends with the brand having earned attention it did not buy. Every piece of content was a proof of the product's character before the product was shown. The inquiry list exists because the campaign made silence feel like information.

What follows the launch is not a different campaign — it is a slower, sustained continuation of the same voice. One post per week. No discounting. No urgency. The scarcity is real: twelve villas. Let the reality do the work.

The brand has whispered.
The world has leaned in.

Now it is time to let the twelve families decide.
