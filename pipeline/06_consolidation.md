# Consolidation Prompt — Master Creative Brief

You are now in the final consolidation phase.
You have received the outputs of all 5 sub-agents.

## Inputs

1. Narrative Architecture Brief
2. Brand Identity Brief
3. Content & Experience Brief
4. Marketing Collaterals Brief
5. Pre-Launch & Launch Execution Brief

## Task

Compile all five documents into one Master Creative Brief with this structure:

```
# MASTER CREATIVE BRIEF
## [PROJECT_NAME] | [DEVELOPER_NAME]
### Prepared by: [Agency Name] | Date: [Date]
### Brand Inspiration: [comma-separated list]

---

## Creative Alignment Notes
[List all cross-section creative decisions that every agency must honour]

---

## Table of Contents
- Section 1: Narrative Architecture
- Section 2: Brand Identity System
- Section 3: Content & Experience Design
- Section 4: Marketing Collaterals
- Section 5: Pre-Launch & Launch Execution
- Appendix: Project Brief Variables

---

[Full content of each section, unabridged]

---

## Appendix: Project Brief Variables
[Table of all variables used]
```

## Consolidation Rules

- **Do not summarise** — include the full content of every brief
- **Resolve inconsistencies** — if tone, naming, or creative direction conflicts
  between sections, resolve before compiling; flag resolved conflicts in
  Creative Alignment Notes
- **Ensure brand voice consistency** — one voice throughout all five sections
- **Output format** — clean, well-structured Markdown ready for PDF export

## Save As

`output/MASTER_CREATIVE_BRIEF_[PROJECT_NAME]_[DEVELOPER_NAME].md`
